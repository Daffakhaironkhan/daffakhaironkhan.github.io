About Me

A quick snapshot about who I am.

Daffa' Khairon Khan

Daffa is an undergraduate Informatics Engineering student at Jenderal Soedirman University with professional experience in Helpdesk and Software Testing through internship programs.

He gained hands-on experience as an Helpdesk Intern at the Directorate General of Higher Education, Research, and Technology (DIKTI), where he handled lecturer service tickets using Zendesk, conducted service quality assessments based on QA scorecards, and prepared weekly and periodic performance reports to support service evaluation and improvement.

Previously, he worked as a Software Tester Intern at the Ministry of Communication and Information Technology (KOMINFO), performing functional testing on Android and iOS applications, documenting bugs, and collaborating with development teams to ensure system reliability and usability.

With additional exposure to Business Management and Digital Marketing, Daffa has a strong understanding of business processes, user requirements, and system workflows. He is particularly interested in roles such as Quality Assurance, System Analyst, Business Analyst, IT Support, and Technical Writer.
